Good news for adventure game fans: _Alfred Pelrock_ is now supported in ScummVM!

Originally released in 1996, _Alfred Pelrock_ is a humorous point-and-click adventure featuring cartoon-style graphics, quirky puzzles, and an irreverent sense of humor. After dreaming of an Egyptian princess imprisoned in the distant past, Alfred embarks on a time-travelling adventure to Ancient Egypt to rescue her, encountering bizarre situations and eccentric characters along the way.

While the game is packed with comedy and satire, players should be aware that it also includes mildly suggestive themes and occasional adult-oriented humor typical of its era. It is also worth noting that the game was released exclusively in Spanish and has no official translations.

As always, if you own an original copy of the game and speak Spanish, grab the latest [daily development build](https://www.scummvm.org/downloads/#daily), add the game’s [required data files](https://wiki.scummvm.org/index.php?title=Alfred_Pelrock:_En_Busca_de_un_Sue%C3%B1o) and enjoy. We also welcome [bug reports](https://bugs.scummvm.org/). and feedback from players to help improve compatibility and preserve this quirky piece of adventure gaming history.
